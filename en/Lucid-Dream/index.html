##method
There are a lot of online games, each with its own way of playing. In fact, you only need to master the core ideas: Dream in a dream, then control dream

  1. When you wake up in the morning, you must record your dreams. Even if you can’t remember any dreams, you must write and draw.
    Normal short-term memory is cleared in 5 minutes, so if you don’t record the dream immediately, the dream will be forgotten instantly
  2. In daily life, remind yourself, “Am I dreaming?”
    Habits can be brought into dreams. The principle can be referred to: The power of habit-why do we live like this and work like this?

For details, please refer to Dream Guide-Analyzing the Mysterious Qingming Dream
