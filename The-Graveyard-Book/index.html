Amusement Park 🎠 Book

Children have no concept of life and death, and the cemetery is more like an amusement park for them

Thank you 🙏 🐼 reader for sending another book

Ashamed, the number of readings plummeted after going abroad
From hundreds of books per year before suddenly to single digits now

Think about the boastful saying that reading should not be utilitarian, but should be driven by interest
Now it’s really slap in the face

But there are also objective reasons:

One is that most of the books I want to read now have original editions
Since there is the original version, I don’t really want to see the translated version

Secondly, I was on a phone call and was heard by a friend from the National University of Singapore
After that, she repeatedly despised “Your Plastic English”

But I have my stubbornness, slow is slow, but I stick to the original version
There should be one day I can get up as fast as Chinese books

The third is that Po County is an island, and there is no such thing as the good mountains and rivers abroad, so lonely as the gods say
And at the beginning of this epidemic, I once thought that this place was a developed country in a mess
In addition, it is summer all year round, except for the hotter, other things are quite comfortable
The blue sky and white clouds, heavy rain and thunder, feel like time is frozen, it’s good

Without utilitarianism, naturally there is no motivation to read

Circuit Breaker is now, so I have some time and mood

I got this book first
I thought it was science fiction at the beginning
I heard that I could be invisible and thought it was fantasy
I thought it was love when I met sister paper
Went to school and thought it was a campus
Dad came to the game and thought it was a parent-child
The whole family was killed and thought it was suspense
In the end, I was talking about growth

As the last author wrote:

I finished the sixth chapter, “Bertie’s School Days” in a shabby hotel in New Orleans. I finished the last page in the gazebo in my garden, holding back tears while writing. Only at that moment, I understood why I didn’t write this book when I was a father. Because before creating, I have to accompany my children through their growth process, nurture them, love them, and finally learn to let go

In addition, it’s hard to get involved in the reading process
The whole book keeps giving me the feeling of a Harry Potter copy

But there is a significant difference in reading this time
In the past, when reading such novels, I often entered the book from the perspective of the protagonist
With age
This time it’s more from Silas’ perspective

If this book is a common fantasy routine in China
Bod will definitely pick up sister Scarlett, tame Sleer, become Master, tear Jack, sit as Brotherhood boss

However, none of this happened in Neil, who wrote about the American Gods.
After Bod played the monster 👾, the sister paper was scared away. Bod is just an ordinary The world is so big, I want to see

As a father, do you want to see your Bod become the one in the country or the one in the book 📖?

There are sometimes stupid things done during the rebellious period that require parents to pay for it
Now that I’m older, I understand the difficulties of the parents’ various prohibitions.

But I can’t help thinking about it
I didn’t know what I knew now

I have imagined that if the self now and the self then
I would definitely not be obedient at that time
You must be hammered in the face by the iron fist of society to wake up
Do something resolutely

After dealing with the general affairs, one day he will step out of the fairy tale world and enter life

“Good-bye, then, Silas.” Bod held out his hand, as he had when he was a small boy, and Silas took it, in a cold hand the color of old ivory, and shook it gravely.
“Good-bye, Nobody Owens.”
Bod picked up the little suitcase. He opened the door to let himself out of the crypt, walked back up the gentle slope to the path without looking back.”

I was miserable when I came out, no one gave me away, no goodbye
The rent in Beijing was refunded and I bought a one-way ticket
All the belongings are packed in three suitcases
Take a taxi to the airport at 4 in the morning
After finishing the consignment, boarding, and then leaving

without looking back

How I wish I also heard Bod’s auditory hallucinations:

He took a step forward … a voice said, “I am so proud of you, my son.”
