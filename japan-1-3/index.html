Osaka

My friend bought a two-day Osaka travel pass early on, so all of the following activities are free 🆓, free 🆓, free 🆓.

Ferris Wheel 🎡

The Redhorse Osaka Wheel has two types of cabins. The white ones are fully transparent, while the red and blue ones have opaque bottoms.

After experiencing the roller coaster 🎢 yesterday, I naturally chose the transparent cabin.

Unlike the Ferris wheel in Singapore 🇸🇬 that is about to close down, you cannot eat inside this one.

One rotation takes about half an hour. I thought we would have to share with others, but it turns out that each person gets their own cabin.

Captain Line

Continuing the view from the boat 🚢, you can see the Ferris wheel 🎡.

Santa Maria

One trip every hour, alternating back and forth, takes about 50 minutes.

Aquarium

There is a huge whale shark model on the wall outside the aquarium.

Touching Fish 🐟

You need to wash your hands, but you can’t use hand sanitizer because it can damage the pH balance of the fish’s skin.

Speaking of which, I have never touched a fish before. The first time, I touched a shark 🦈.

It was different from what I imagined. It was warm and soft, like touching a girl’s leg.

Unique drink at the aquarium 🥤. It’s the blue color of the ocean and tastes really good. After coming out, I had teppanyaki.

HEP FIVE Ferris Wheel

The special thing about this Ferris wheel 🎡 is that it is inside a shopping mall. I saw Gundam 🤖️ models and Fate.

By the way, after staying at the Marriott in Rome 🇷🇴 last time, I naturally became familiar with Hilton.

I have to say that on the Ferris wheel, among the sparkling night view 🌃, the Hilton sign shone brightly.

Airport ✈️

When the flight attendant checked my ticket, she asked if I was alone. I was puzzled. Of course, I am alone.

Then I saw her perform a series of actions, and congratulated me on the upgrade.

At that time, I was really confused and received the ticket 🎫 without any reaction. The flight attendant saw my expressionless face and thought I didn’t understand, so she repeated it.

However, there is a difference between an upgraded ticket and a self-paid one. Unlike my first visit to Japan 🇯🇵, with an upgraded ticket, I could not enter the VIP lounge ♨️.

So this time, I couldn’t take a bath 🛀, but the service I enjoyed on the plane was exactly the same.
