Replica of the Statue of Liberty

To commemorate the “Japanese French Year”, from April 1998 to May 1999, the French Statue of Liberty from Cygnes Island came to Odaiba, Tokyo. [3] Due to its popularity, a replica of the French Statue of Liberty was erected in the same place in 2000. Also in Japan, a small Statue of Liberty is in the Amerika-mura (American Village) shopping district in Osaka, Japan. Another replica is in Oirase[71] near Shimoda Town in the southern part of Misawa City, Aomori Prefecture, a US Air Force base with 8,000 people. A replica of the Statue of Liberty in Ishinomaki City, Miyagi Prefecture, was damaged by the 2011 Tohoku earthquake and tsunami. [72] There is also a replica in Oyabe City, Toyama Prefecture. [73]
