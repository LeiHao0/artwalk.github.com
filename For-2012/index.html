To the dead 2012

Posted on 2013-03-27 Edited on 2020-12-21 Disqus: Symbols count in article: 5.5k Reading time ≈ 5 mins.

I still have a hindsight. It was originally a New Year’s Day summary. It was delayed until the Spring Festival, and then today.

Written in 2012

I was restless a month before the Spring Festival
I don’t know if it has something to do with a person outside
When I went home during the Chinese New Year, I looked at the snow flying in the sky and the earth wrapped in silver on the bus, and my heart returned to peace.

2012 was the year I grew the most
Leaving aside the changes in the external environment, we can no longer use students’ standards to demand ourselves

In the first half of the year, I had nothing to do, read books and played games at home
Remember the godlike work “Take Time as a Friend”
The heart is eager, and I read it intermittently for a month
After that, the world feels different
Probably since then, fell in love with reading

Overkill

I still remember the process of Tai Chi from rigid to soft in my sophomore year. I chatted with my teacher once
He said that I was shaking too hard, and asked my classmates to take a video with their mobile phones, and I would understand by looking at them.
I was stunned and said, I thought this was the only way to go.

Yes, this is overkill

I also felt back pain when I was in high school
I have been exposed to traditional medicine since then
But recently I felt pain in my limbs and neck pillow
I heard a sentence in Dongwu’s Theory of Relativity, “When you realize his existence, it may be a problem.”
I thought it was more serious and wondered if I was going to die
But recently I saw it in “The Art of Learning”, it may also be an introduction to Tai Chi, and I felt something I didn’t feel before
In short, everything goes on and stays calm

At a glance

Sometimes it’s so strange, you can notice her at a glance when you are in a crowd
Even if it’s not the first sight, after a while, in the vast crowd, you will notice this person
Just seeing it can distinguish her from the people around her
Background, personality, occupation, hobbies are all unknown, but you can still feel her special

Go to the Westside Ice Rink on Saturday, this is the second time in my life skating
Like skating
One is to make yourself feel the difficulties of being an upright animal again and train your cerebellum
The second is to re-understand the balance, this and Tai Chi should have a complementary effect
So decided to make it a constant for weekend sports
At present, friction is still used for steering, and I hope to use centripetal force next time

impetuous

Everyone is saying that this is an impetuous society

Yes, the general trend
There is a saying: If you live in an abnormal place for a long time, you will regard the abnormality as justified.

I do not deny that I am impetuous
I want to do everything, I didn’t get into anything
But I feel helpless. Reading and paying attention only started from the senior year.
Knowing GTD, what’s the use
The key is the quality of time usage, not the quantity
flow- heart flow, time stops for you

The same is true for impetuousness, I feel like how to deal with a deep and broad relationship
First wide-ranging, as Qiao said, keep trying to discover, and eventually, you will find your interest
I have been struggling with programming for two years in the university. I naively thought it was a lifetime thing.
And thought that as long as you are called a master or a great person, you don’t have to worry about money
And claim to work for interest, not money
Look now, it was so silly and naive
In the carefree four years of college, I didn’t make it wide. Although it’s a bit late now, I still have to continue to do this.

Moving

Thank you to those friends in Hefei (I turned out to be disgusted with this term, but later found out that there is another meaning, Chinese culture is really broad and profound), I used to pull you out every time I was sad.
And stay with me for a while
Who else, when I learned that I had five subjects and retaken one, did not get a bachelor’s degree in psychology, did not enter a graduate school, out of desperation for a humble salary and rushed around, and prepared the day before my birthday When you are hit and sad and messed up, there is no disgust but comfort

Sanshengshi

Just listening to the name is awesome

The first contact was in “Sou Shen Ji”:

Lie on the three-life stone, realize the past and next lives, drink the worry-free spring water, and forget the grudges

Last time I visited Hangzhou and I googled Hangzhou attractions, I found three words “Sanshengshi”

At the end of my 22-year-old, there were many things that deviated from my vision
And I found that everything I did was probably avoiding (what to avoid?)

The most depressing thing is that after a sleep, the brain recompiles and runs everything according to what it wants.

When I woke up, I found myself sleeping very deep and relaxed, just like the time when I heard Zhao tell me I have BL in my junior year.

Then I went to see the Sanshengshi, not for anything else, after all, it’s my name to remove the life.

Utility and freedom

Understand true liberalism, understand the role of inheritance tax,

I understand that I may not have independent views, and there is a huge gap between critical thinking and critical thinking.

I understand that since “Happiness Lesson”, I feel so good about myself, and even overcorrected

Understand that you still can’t make promises to others, no matter who that person is

I understand that in order not to disappoint myself or others, I frequently choose not to act

Understand that sometimes opportunities are used to wait and change, the final result is lost

I understand that once I get frustrated, I still feel sad

But I still don’t understand, what when where who why how

Sometimes I feel that everything I do is just a small mess, so what if I change it, I still feel a sense of powerlessness

Yuyu stream bottom pine, seedlings from the mountain
In the diameter of the stem, shade this hundred feet

Wan said she recently discovered that if you work hard, you will achieve it

But my model is, it may not be realized, at least there will be many gaps between the real result and my own modeling

hope

Of course, I think, maybe they are just trying to excuse themselves and find a good-sounding reason for themselves
Of course, more likely, what I said is wrong
But in any case, no matter how you sigh, the wonderful 22 has passed.
Sometimes it feels like there is a clock, ticking on the alarm clock
Is it an inner time observation, but also the experience of the passage of time
It’s true, the dead are like husbands

I also know that hope does not matter whether there is or nothing
But I can’t help but hope that I’m on my way to the end of the third cycle.
I can make a breakthrough, just like the name of a Microsoft game: The Rise of the Country
