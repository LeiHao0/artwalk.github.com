Dream interpretation

Posted on 2014-04-15 Edited on 2020-12-21 Disqus:
Symbols count in article: 3k Reading time ≈ 3 mins.

The little mage found himself doing some tricks
The needle is stuck on the back of the hand, inside the red blood needle tube
Without thinking, I pressed the C (pause?) key

I breathe tightly, and I feel choked instantly. It feels like I am in a vacuum, and the air in my body leaks out of the pinhole.
The little mage was terrified and hurriedly pressed the pause button again

After about a few seconds, the little mage took a sigh of relief and finally felt that he could breathe.
He took a few deep breaths. Before he thought that “If you die in a catastrophe, you will have a blessing.” He discovered a serious problem-no heartbeat

This time, the little mage was scared
Oh my god, how could this happen, the little mage thought to himself, there is breathing but no pulse, what’s the matter with swelling?

Acute cardiac arrest is one of the most important causes of death in the elves
Fortunately, the little mage was in the celestial dynasty, he did not give up the treatment, and proceeded to perform CPR on himself. At this time, he was already lying on the bed.

However, there are several key points for high-quality CPR:

  • Compression rate is at least 100 compressions per minute
  • Adults compress at least 5 cm
  • Ensure chest rebound after each compression
  • Minimize the interruption of chest compressions
  • Avoid hyperventilation

In this way, the current situation of the little mage is simply a joke

In addition, no blood circulation means that there is no energy exchange with oxygen, and the body’s activities gradually stop.
But I can move, still doing chest compressions on myself, and have such a clear will
Although the little mage was puzzled, he did not stop to treat himself.

Working hard for a while, then he woke up…

It’s late at night, and there’s no sound around it
Whenever he has a nightmare, when the little mage wakes up, he often finds that his hand is pressed against the position of the heart, but this time it is on the right.

I think that when I was in the first grade of the Master Academy, the little mage was exposed to hypnosis, and he was very relaxed and comfortable after doing it. Later, I listened to it once before going to bed every night, and the quality of sleep soared.

By chance, the little mage turned to an ancient book Uncover the mystery of the human mind
It’s a pity that this book is too thick. When the deadline for returning the book is reached, the little mage can only read half of it

But the cheats are the cheats. For example, Mei Chaofeng only got half of the Jiuyin Scripture, which is enough to kill Qiu Chuji and other old ways in seconds.

The little mage was stunned by Freund’s Oedipus theory, but “the dream is to achieve a certain wish” inspired him
Some dreams can be found out by themselves

Thanks to the seemingly scientific explanation, coupled with the bonus of hypnosis halo, the little mage’s dreams gradually disappear

PS: A few years later, the little mage flipped through this book again, read the remaining half, and saw the description of controlling dreams, although only one sentence:

Some people can clearly know that they are sleeping and dreaming at night, so they seem to have the ability to guide dreams with their will. For example, when this kind of dreamer is not satisfied with the dream, he can interrupt the dream without waking up, and then start in a new direction.

The little mage can only sigh that good luck makes people, it is very close to this, if you insisted on going through it back then, you might not have made so many detours.
There is another thing in the same way, I will talk about it later when I have a chance [pit]
