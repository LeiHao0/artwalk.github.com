Japan-2-0

🇯🇵

sequence

Little Master’s eldest sister suddenly said to go to Hakone

So skip some (many) chapters and write this first

As for why it became traditional
Because the eldest sister is Taiwanese
Although she can understand simplified characters

It happened in 8102 3🈷️

At that time, the little mage had served in the former company for 3.5 years, and then went to the fruit company to sweep the floor 🧹, and his birthday 🎂 was on the same day as the pit
I plan to give myself a run similar to graduation 🎓 travel before that

After all, it’s very troublesome to change jobs, and being an inferior person will definitely be humiliated.
So I plan to prepare both mentally and physically in advance

At that time, the little mage was still young and was still in the stage of wanting to go out to play. The way to relax is that the world is so big I want to see
So looking at the world map, I feel like I want to go everywhere

But because it’s Solo, I just got my driver’s license and I didn’t dare to drive before the internship period.
Naturally, Europe and the United States can’t think about it, but choose the urban landscape
Let’s take a look at the 3-year return visa that I finally got, so I can only go to 🇯🇵

In view of the fact that the last time the little mage found in Kinosaki that the fake hot spring was boiler water, and the real hot spring ♨️ can heal a disease, he would never forget to go 🇯🇵 soak once

So the route is all the way north

Hakone-Kamakura Enoshima-Yokohama-Tokyo

Although I have been to Tokyo, because the first time I was following others, the little mage had a poor memory, and it was only 6 days short, so I had no impression.
So exactly as expected, when I came to Tokyo again, apart from knowing Skytree, it seemed like I had never been to other places.

Odakyu

This time it’s JAL, landing at Narita Airport 🛬️
Originally, the young master planned to go to Shinjuku and transfer to the Odakyu Romantic Express (it is said that it is not romantic) and go directly to Hakone
After all, it’s only 4 o’clock, and the last Odakyu train is 6 o’clock. Even if you go from Narita to Shinjuku, it’s completely too late.
However hungry for Japan 🇯🇵 time is an hour earlier, the little mage is dumbfounded after the airport time synchronization

In a panic, the enthusiastic old man in front helped buy a ticket for the Narita Express to Shinagawa to Shinkansen 🚄 under the condition of language barrier. 🎫

Catch the Guangming, which resembles the train man, is very stable and comfortable. You can see the location on the Map and jump forward quickly.
At Odawara (Odawara), the sun has already set, there is no bus, only the mountain train

After leaving the station, I was confused and didn’t know where to go. Many people walked towards me, only to see a beautiful little sister. I quickly went up and asked where the little train was.
After the little sister gave the way gently, the little mage walked a long way and looked back and saw the little sister nodding to indicate that he had found the right carriage.

After going up the mountain, it gradually rained and the temperature dropped sharply. The little mage found that the seat was getting hot, and heating was blowing up.
Really humane

After arriving at Kowakidani, the watermelon 🍉 card balance is insufficient to leave the station
There is no one around, just when the little mage wants to turn over and will be caught
An Officer appeared from nowhere, gave him a piece of paper, opened the subway door, and disappeared

I saw a hill when I went out of the station, and after repeatedly consulting the map, I still couldn’t believe that the vertical ladder hidden in the shadow was the road shown on the map.

Fortunately, after climbing the stairs, I arrived on a normal national road
Only 1.3km uphill

The little mage is holding an umbrella and dragging his luggage, walking alone on the road
Passing by a big 7-11
Still not alone

…

Tenyu

When the two characters Tianyou appeared in the rainy night, the little mage had no energy

I thought I could enjoy the hot spring
I was hungry for a long time and couldn’t find the hotel entrance
Had to call the hotel

At that time, the little mage’s English was still very poor, and coupled with the other party’s Japanese English, he couldn’t understand what was being said at all, so he could only say it by himself.

Then I was told not to move and I would send a car to pick him up
After waiting for a while, there was no movement, so I had to continue walking up the mountain road.
During that time, a car came down from the mountain and experienced no one. The first thought of the little mage was, there is a car on the mountain? !

Give way to the bottom and continue to go up

After walking for a while, the little mage suddenly opened his mouth, it wouldn’t be the car just now.

Go back quickly

Back to the bus stop just now, I found that the car really stopped there
After showing the Booking Detail with the driver, he picked up the Master

In the meantime, I asked the driver if there was any Shuttle at Xiaotianyuan Station.
The answer is too far no
I don’t understand how to find this station
There are just a few bus stops nearby, found by name

The most let the little mage vomit blood is that the car returned on the same route as it just came back. It only went 500m further, and a U-turn arrived at the hotel entrance…

It’s not that the little mage is blind, it’s really too dark and it’s raining to see the road.

So if you go to Hakone from Narita Airport, don’t miss Odakyu
