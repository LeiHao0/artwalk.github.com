In the next steps, you are using vim, which I think is the hardest part.

  1. Press i and you will see --INSERT-- in the lower left corner of Terminal.
  2. Paste 00 10 25 3 * zsh ~/Documents/sendiMessage.sh
  3. Press shift + : and enter wq, enter
