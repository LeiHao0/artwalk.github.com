So I was full of good feelings in an instant, and I went through most of the videos of the year and benefited a lot.

Later learned that as long as there is a developer account, Visa/MC credit card, you can draw a lottery
Register at official website at the beginning of March every year. There is only one week, so please don’t miss it

Due to the large number of people,  will randomly select some lucky (reimbursed by the company) or unlucky (own expense) leeks to give 10,000 tickets 🎫
Afterwards, whether to go or not, deduct the money first
No matter if the U.S. visa is refused or denied entry, the money will not be refunded
Tickets are also forbidden to transfer and can only be sent to other developer accounts of the same company
In short, don’t even think about refunding the lottery, so think about it clearly
